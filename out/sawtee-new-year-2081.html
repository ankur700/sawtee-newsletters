Dear friends and colleagues

As we embark upon a new year and reflect on the accomplishments of the past year, we are excited to share the accomplishments made by SAWTEE in advancing its mission on research, capacity-building, and advocacy at both national and regional levels.

Among the notable accomplishments, SAWTEE produced two studies: one examining the Nepal-India Trade Treaty and avenues for amendments, and the other on “The 66/77 products inside out: The long and short of the United States’ Nepal Trade Preference Programme”.

Additionally, SAWTEE convened discussion programmes on topics such as the public debt situation in select South Asian countries, the prospects and challenges of Nepal's regional trade, and bilateral trade relations with key partners like the United States and China. SAWTEE continued to provide inputs on different trade and development issues to the private sector, government and international organizations.

This year, SAWTEE started hosting a Reform Monitoring Platform, with support from USAID Trade and Competitiveness, on its website. The Platform is an initiative to strengthen monitoring and evaluation of the trade- and investment-related policy reform process in Nepal.

In line with its commitment to capacity building, SAWTEE offered workshops to media professionals in Koshi, Madhesh, Bagmati and Karnali provinces. Aiming to strengthen the skills and knowledge of economic journalists, SAWTEE also offered media fellowship to five Nepali journalists, enriching their expertise and understanding in the field of international trade and economics. Further, SAWTEE continued its publication of Trade Insight , policy briefs and issue briefs on varied topics.

Thank you for your continued support. We look forward to continuing our efforts in the coming year in the areas covering trade, economics and environment. If you have any feedback, please reach out to us through sawtee@sawtee.org or our social media accounts.

We wish you a happy, healthy, and prosperous New Year 2081.
